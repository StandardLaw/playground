About

It is difficult to transmit banking account information, proof of age, education qualifications, healthcare data, and other sorts of verified personal information via the Web. These sorts of data are often referred to as verifiable claims.

The mission of the Verifiable Claims group at W3C is to make expressing, exchanging, and verifying claims easier, privacy-enhancing, and more secure on the Web. Come and help us make the Web a better place.
